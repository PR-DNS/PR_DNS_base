SCALED_REDIST_PARAMS  params

This is an independent call for redistribution and optimization of the interface mesh. A parallel communication of front should be called following this call to ensure that the redistribution is consistent globally. The function will redistribute all curves and surfaces (3D). It will recursively do either 10 times or when nothing to be done.
