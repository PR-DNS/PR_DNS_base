This function retrieves the left and right states at a point. Since a point can be shared by different entities, the associated hyper surface element (bond in 2D and tri in 3D) and hyper surface (curve in 2D and surface in 3D) must be provided as input.

Parameters
pin Pointer to point.
hsein Pointer to the associated hyper surface element.
hsin Pointer to the associated hyper surface.
slout Address for the left state.
srout Address for the right state.
